# S023.16 - Curated Starter Curriculum Seed

## Epic

Epic 5 - Lessons and Learner Experience (parent: [S023](./00-overview.md))

## Status

Done

## Goal

Replace the broad extracted content seed with a small, curated starter curriculum that is suitable for children, diaspora families, beginner learners, audio-first learning, and image-supported lessons.

## Scope

- Seed English, French, and Médumba language rows.
- Seed 80 starter concepts across 10 beginner lessons.
- Seed English and French concept texts for every starter concept.
- Seed Médumba concept texts only where the extracted Sounglah source data already has a matching candidate.
- Seed 10 published lessons and 80 active vocabulary lesson items.
- Add an explicit destructive reset option for local reseeding: `flask --app run.py seed --reset`.

## Content Notes

The starter curriculum includes:

1. Greetings & Kindness
2. My Family
3. Food & Eating
4. Home & Daily Actions
5. Emotions & Encouragement
6. Numbers & Counting
7. Animals Around Us
8. Nature & Weather
9. School & Learning
10. Community & Culture

Médumba rows seeded from extracted source data are marked `needs_review` and include a usage note saying they require native review. No new Médumba text was invented.

## Verification

- Local reseed should be run from `backend/` with `flask --app run.py seed --reset`.
