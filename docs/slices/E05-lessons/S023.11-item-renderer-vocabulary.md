# S023.11 - Lesson Item Renderer: VOCABULARY

## Epic

Epic 5 - Lessons and Learner Experience (parent: [S023](./00-overview.md))

## Status

Planned

## Goal

First real learner screen: teach one word/idea with image, Médumba text, English/French support text, and play audio.

## Dependencies

- S023.10 (player shell).
- Public play payload includes resolved concept data per item.

## Public API enrichment

Full payload shape and resolver rules: [00-overview.md § Public Play Payload Contract](./00-overview.md#public-play-payload-contract).

Use `conceptPayload.texts.med.audioUrl` with existing **Mini Audio Player** (S014.7).

## UI Contract

- Concept image (large, rounded, warm frame).
- **Médumba** phrase prominently (heritage-first typography).
- Play audio button for Médumba (primary interaction).
- Show EN and FR translations (from `conceptPayload.texts`) for parent/grandma context alongside heritage text.
- Optional `instructionText` from lesson item above content.

### Edge cases

- Missing image: illustration placeholder with Sounglah palette.
- Missing audio: disabled play + “Audio coming soon” (not a hard error).
- Missing translation: omit line, don’t show empty labels.

## Out Of Scope

- Recording, quizzes, tap-to-reveal games.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Greeting Grandma item 1 (Grandma) renders real concept content when concepts are complete.
- Audio plays when approved recording exists.
- Works on mobile width ~375px.
- i18n for chrome; concept text comes from CMS.
- Lint/build pass.

## Verification

Manual: play step 1 with seeded grandmother concept.

cd frontend
