## Goal

## Dependencies

- S023.1 through S023.14 complete (or all renderers stubbed except known gaps documented).

## Human Content Required (Ask Before Seeding)

**The builder must stop and ask the human** for the following before writing seed data or marking this slice done:

1. **Médumba text** for `grandmother` (and optional pronunciation).
2. **Médumba phrase** for `hello_grandma` — the exact wording a child would say to grandma.
3. **English and French** equivalents if not already in Concept Texts.
4. **Confirmation** that Médumba audio has been recorded and approved for vocabulary and phrase steps (or explicit OK to ship with “Audio coming soon” for QA).

Do not invent heritage-language copy. Placeholder Latin or machine translation is not acceptable for seed content.
